## The Challenge

Mario's Italian Kitchen, a staple in Downtown Water City, faced constant drain backups during peak dinner service. The 40-year-old cast iron pipes were corroding, causing slow drainage and foul odors that threatened their health inspection rating.

<Callout type="danger" title="Critical Deadline">
The local health inspector flagged the issue and gave the restaurant a 72-hour deadline to resolve the drainage failures or face closure.
</Callout>

## Why This Project Succeeded

Key factors that enabled us to complete this complex project in just 48 hours:

- **Pre-staged materials**: All PVC, fittings, and the grease trap were ordered and delivered before demolition began
- **Two-shift operation**: Day and night crews kept work continuous
- **Quick-cure concrete**: Specialty fast-setting concrete allowed floor restoration within hours
- **Parallel workflows**: While excavation continued in one area, pipe installation began in completed sections

<Callout type="default" title="Lesson Learned">
Commercial kitchen plumbing is complex and heavily regulated. Always work with a team that has specific commercial kitchen experience and understands health code requirements.
</Callout>
